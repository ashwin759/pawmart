# Full Prompt: Redesign UI + Vibrant Color Theme + Light/Dark Mode Optimization

## Objective

Completely upgrade the **Pet Marketplace (PawMart) UI** so it looks like a **professional commercial product** instead of an AI-generated template.

The new design must be:

• visually engaging
• vibrant and modern
• emotionally appealing (pet friendly)
• startup-level professional
• optimized for **Light Mode and Dark Mode**

Maintain performance, usability, and accessibility.

---

# 1. Redesign the Hero Section

Improve the current hero layout.

Requirements:

• Replace the single centered pet image with **multiple layered pet images**
• Add subtle gradient shapes in the background
• Include playful pet-themed visual elements (paw shapes or rounded shapes)

Hero content:

Headline:
Bring Home Your Perfect Pet Companion

Subtext:
Discover pets, learn about breeds, and find the perfect companion for your family.

Buttons:

Primary:
Browse Pets

Secondary:
Find Your Match

Hero should feel **energetic and welcoming**.
