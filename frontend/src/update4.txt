# Prompt: Light Mode Redesign + Mobile Optimization (Professional & Commercial UI)

## Objective

Upgrade the **Light Mode UI** to a **rich, modern, and visually appealing commercial design**, and ensure the entire application is **fully optimized for mobile devices**.

The UI must feel:

• clean and premium
• vibrant but balanced
• easy to read
• responsive on all devices
• consistent across all components

---

# 1. Light Mode Design Direction

Redesign the light theme to avoid a dull or flat look.

Use:

• soft layered backgrounds
• subtle color variations
• premium spacing
• modern shadows

Avoid:

• plain white everywhere
• low contrast text
• overly bright or childish colors
